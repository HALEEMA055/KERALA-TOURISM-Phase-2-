Kerala tourism is renowned world over for its exotic destinations, cultural heritage and unparalleled hospitality. Tourism in Kerala attracts millions of tourists every year from all parts of the world. From pristine beaches, scenic hill stations, wildlife sanctuaries to ancient temples, churches and palaces, Kerala tourism offers everything that a tourist can desire on a fulfilling holiday. Kerala Tourism has travel information centers all over the state, which assist and provide information to travellers about all of Kerala's tourist destinations, hotels, transport and travel guidelines. The informative website of Kerala tourism is the ultimate Kerala tourism guide, which offers in-depth details about Kerala tourism, Kerala travel destinations, Kerala accommodations, Kerala holiday packages, Kerala backwater cruises and other travel related services. Kerala Tourism Department is known for its innovative and market focused Ad campaigns.
